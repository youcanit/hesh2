Our finances, your money?

Our intention is to have NO profit. All the money that members pay for events, returns to the members themselves. The fixed management (Marylou, Johan) and organizing committee of a particular edition have not any material or financial interest in doing this. They are doing this gladly and 100 % on voluntary basis, for free.

We are a 100 % non-profit organization. However, we DO have some savings. Currently, we have accumulated 3084 euro from the 5 previous editions. This amount originates from:

  • healthy budget management (if you don't make profit, you probably make loss)
  • from certain participants who signed up but finally couldn‚Äôt participate. In such situation, in line with our charter, these members become involuntary sponsors.

These savings will - in hte end - alwasy return entirely to all members.

They are used for:

  • Funding the preparation of the next edition (e.g., paying the advance deposit to the hotel)
  • Sponsoring partially the hotel/rego fees: members will pay less than the actual cost.
  • Maintaining a minimum buffer for unexpected events/expenses.

How do we choose the location of our events?

The choice of a next edition typically happens through a kind of voting at the end of a HESH event.

Candidate-organizers present and promote in front of all participants their proposal (the proposed location, the proposed organization team etc) and all present participants can bring out 1 (secret) vote. The proposal which gets the most votes, wins the election, and gets the honour to organize the next edition.
