Where?

Bruges, Belgium. The Venice of the north!

Bruges has a rich history, it's center is Unesco world heritage. It's a vibrant city with lots of culture like festivals, museums. It's probably the most famous Belgian city, known world-wide. Before and/or after our event, you can use it as a base to visit things in the area, such as the Belgian coast (which is nearby), nature reserves (the Zwin) , other famous cities like Brussels (European capital), cosmopolitan Antwerp, historic Ghent. You can also do nature trekking, for example in the Ardennes. Everything in Belgium is close because Belgium is a rather small country.
