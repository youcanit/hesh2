Our membership policy

HESH is a CLOSED group of friends. It means: you must be accepted as new member. You might wrongly think that we are just another HASH event (like there are so many across the world) where everyone who wants it, can participate.

We want to keep the group limited in size, say up to max 30 active like-minded souls. Because, first of all, we want to keep it cozy and guard that “everyone knows everyone”. This is obviously a minimum requirement to consider each other as friends.

Moreover, too many members would make the organization of our regular events a headache for the organizers. Imagine for instance trying to find a place to have diner together, if you would be a group of 30 people. A cumbersome organization would be the beginning of the end: nobody would any longer volunteer to organize the next edition!
